About MereSaathPadhlo

MereSaathPadhlo is created with one simple goal — to help students study smarter, not harder. Education should not feel stressful, confusing, or expensive. Our platform provides free study materials, previous question papers, notes, and helpful learning resources so students can prepare confidently and easily.

We understand how difficult it is to find proper resources during exams. That’s why we are building this platform step by step, based on what students need the most.

Our Mission

To provide free, simple, and easy-to-understand learning resources for every student.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted platform for academic preparation and self-learning.

Why We Built This

  • Students struggle to find correct notes
  • Most study resources are paid
  • Students waste time searching instead of learning

Here, everything is designed by students, for students.

Thank you for being a part of MereSaathPadhlo. Together, let’s make learning simple.
